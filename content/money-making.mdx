# Making Money with Your Tech Blog

So you've built a tech blog. Now what? How do you turn your code tutorials into cash?

## 1. Affiliate Marketing

This is the easiest way to start. Recommend tools you actually use.

- Hosting (Vercel, Netlify)
- Domain Registrars
- SaaS Tools (Notion, Linear)

## 2. Ad Networks

Once you have traffic, you can join ad networks.

- **Google AdSense**: Good for beginners.
- **Carbon Ads**: Targeted for developers.

## 3. Selling Digital Products

Create an ebook or a course.

> "The best investment you can make is in yourself." - Warren Buffett

## Conclusion

Start writing, keep shipping, and the money will follow.
